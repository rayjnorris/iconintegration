Public Employee Retirement System of Idaho and ICON celebrate the completion of their Data Project

September 30, 2016

ICON concluded another successful project recently when the Public Employee Retirement System of Idaho (PERSI) went live with their new benefit administration system. ICON led the effort to provide high quality data that was migrated into the new system. This deployment represented the culmination of many years of data analysis, data profiling, data cleansing, data transformation, data migration and data reconciliation services from ICON.
