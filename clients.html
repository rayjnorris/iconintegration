Clients

Public Employees Retirement Association of New Mexico (NM PERA)

NM PERA hired ICON Integration and Design in August of 2016 to provide expert data services, including, data analysis, data assessment, data cleansing, and data consulting. NM PERA is refreshing their current benefit administration system and addressing known data issues that have caused NM PERA staff to perform manual work arounds and exert additional effort. NM PERA is a statewide public pension system with over 100,000 active and retired members and over 700 reporting employers.

Texas County and District Retirement System (TCDRS)

TCDRS has engaged ICON Integration and Design since July 2015 to assist in their COMPASS system replacement project. ICON provided an initial Data Conversion Project plan and continues to provide data assessment, data reconciliation, and data testing services. TCDRS is a large statewide system that administers a wide range of benefit plans for nearly 700 employers and over 300,000 active and retired members.

Virginia Retirement System (VRS)

VRS has used ICON Integration and Design on two separate engagements. From the fall of 2009 through deployment in late 2012 ICON performed a full data project in support of VRS installing the first phase of a new benefits administration system. This data project used the full ICON methodology to assess, profile, cleanse, transform, migrate, and reconcile data from the legacy systems to the new benefit administration system. Beginning in January 2014 ICON was asked to perform a second full data project at VRS as they developed the remaining features of their new benefit administration system. This project is ongoing. VRS is one of the largest public pension systems in the country with half a million active and retired members and over 800 employers.
