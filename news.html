Public Employee Retirement System of Idaho and ICON celebrate the completion of their Data Project
September 30, 2016

ICON concluded another successful project recently when the Public Employees Retirement System of Idaho (PERSI) went live with their new benefit administration system. ICON led the effort to provide high quality data that was migrated into the new system. This deployment represented the culmination of many years of data analysis, data profiling, data cleansing, data transformation, data migration and data reconciliation services from ICON.

Public Employees Retirement Association of New Mexico hires ICON
August 30, 2016

The Public Employees Retirement Association of New Mexico (NM PERA) recently hired ICON Integration and Design to provide expert data services, including, data analysis, data assessment, data cleansing, and data consulting. NM PERA is refreshing their current benefit administration system and addressing known data issues that have caused NM PERA staff to perform manual work arounds and exert additional effort. NM PERA is a statewide public pension system with over 100,000 active and retired members and over 700 reporting employers.
