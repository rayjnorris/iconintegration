About Us

ICON Integration and Design offers Strategic Information Technology Solutions and Services globally.

ICON focuses on data and the processes affecting data quality and integrity.  ICON does not pretend to be “all-things to everyone”, we work directly with clients and vendors to provide premiere data solutions.

ICON has extensive industry experience in:

  • Pension 
  • Healthcare
  • Financial
  • Insurance
  • Telecommunications
  • ‍ Manufacturing

ICON’S staff consists of experienced information technology professionals with certifications in: Project Management, Oracle Database Administration, Microsoft System Engineering, MS SQL Server, IBM DB2, IBM Mid-Range AS/400, Six Sigma, and ITIL. Our staff reflects the company’s commitment to providing expert data solutions. ICON’S seasoned technical organization has remarkable depth, the technical group averages 15+ years of experience working in an IT environment.

ICON Integration and Design supplies expertise in the following areas:

  • Assessment of current IT platforms against business goals‍
  • ‍Data quality assessment and cleansing
  • ‍Transition of data to new operating systems while improving ease of use
  • ‍Data Management staffing and project management
  • Due diligence and assessment of IT data prior to a merger or acquisition
  • Design and implementation of integration projects for two or more disparate systems
  • Providing on-going consulting services for data experts such as database administrators, data modelers, project managers, and data/business analysts.

When it comes to data quality, cleansing, migration, and integration – ICON Integration and Design provides the expertise to ensure your organization’s data needs are handled professionally and on schedule.
